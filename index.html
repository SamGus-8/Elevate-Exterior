FAQ

What homeowners usually ask first.

Will pressure washing damage my home?

Not when the right method is used. Many exterior surfaces are better served with soft washing, which uses lower pressure and the proper cleaning solution to remove buildup without forcing high pressure against siding, trim, or delicate materials.

Do I need to be home?

In many cases, no. As long as we have access to the areas being cleaned and any needed water access, we can complete the service and communicate with you before and after the job.

How do I receive a quote?

You can text or upload photos of the surfaces you want cleaned. We review the photos, consider size and condition, and follow up with a clear quote and recommended service plan.

Do you clean and seal more than driveways?

Yes. We clean driveways, house exteriors, sidewalks, patios, fences, decks, and other exterior surfaces depending on the material and condition. We can also quote concrete sealing for driveways, patios, and walkways.

Do you offer concrete sealing?

Yes. Concrete sealing is available for qualifying concrete surfaces after cleaning. Sealing helps protect against moisture, stains, and everyday wear while keeping the surface easier to maintain.
